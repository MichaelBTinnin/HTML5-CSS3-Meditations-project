Mission Statement:

Michael's Meditation is a nonprofit organization devoted to spreading the practice of meditation

We have online meditations and in person classe to help those interested grow

We believe that meditation is useful to everyone and that involvement with our organization benefits all involved

We invite all of those interested to take advantage of our classes and seminars

We believe that developing a meditation practice is a lifelong journey that can change people's lives in great ways

We strive to build community through the growth and development that meditation provides
